About Us

The HWFT Foundation, hosted at Macrowave.org, is dedicated to advancing Harald Wave Field Theory (HWFT). Our mission is to unlock the multidimensional dynamics of energy and matter through pioneering wave-based research.

Our Research Initiatives

Emotional-Intent Intelligence (EII)

Developing AI frameworks that interpret emotional and intent signals through wave patterns to enhance human–AI collaboration.

Macrowave Theory

Studying wave coherence at cosmic and quantum levels, redefining energy, gravity, and communication systems.

Applied Technologies

Turning HWFT concepts into tangible innovations—from propulsion systems to resonance-based energy storage.

Vision & Values

We envision a world where understanding wave fields empowers humanity to explore space sustainably, heal ecosystems, and create new paradigms in science and technology—all guided by curiosity, ethics, and global collaboration.
